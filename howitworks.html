How it works

Dashboard

Registered commuters have a dashboard from which all activities are monitored, number of trips included. Users can view/hide their wallet balance, view inbox on promotions from ARRIVE.




Connect with Driver

This buttons pushes you to make a search for any available driver going to your preferred destination.

Find a Ride

Search destination.

Type in your preferred destination on the map and hit the search button.

View Drivers.

View available drivers en route to your preferred destination.

Select available drivers.

View drivers’ rating and select your preferred driver.

Driver Profile

Here, you can find information about your driver, driver rating, number of trips and vehicle type amongst others.




Ride Details

Details about the ride - Vehicle capacity, passengers aboard, empty seats, ride fare, driver arrival time as well as destination time.
