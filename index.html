Frequently Asked Questions

  • ARRIVE is a ride-sharing app based in Nigeria that connects riders with nearby drivers to provide affordable and reliable transportation.

  • To sign up for ARRIVE, download the app from the App Store or Google Play, fill in your personal information, and complete the verification process. You can either sign up as a Driver or Sign up as a passanger.

  • To book a ride with ARRIVE, simply open the app, input your pickup location and destination, select your preferred ride option, and tap the "Connect with Driver" button.

  • ARRIVE's fare prices are calculated based on the distance of the ride, time of day, and local demand.

  • ARRIVE performs background checks on all its drivers and has in-app safety features such as in-app emergency support, real-time GPS tracking, and two-way ratings system.
