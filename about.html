About Us

We’re redefining movement, making it
faster and cheaper.

Welcome to ARRIVE, designed to reduce crowding at bus stations and make your commute more comfortable and convenient. We understand the frustration of dealing with long lines and crowded buses, which is why we created this app to make your daily commute a breeze.

Our Mission

"Our mission is to create a system that decongests the crowd at the bus stops by pairing 4-5 "potential passengers" to one ride going in the same route".

Our Vision

"At ARRIVE, our vision is to reduce overcrowdedness in stations and on public transport by matching people with similar commutes."
